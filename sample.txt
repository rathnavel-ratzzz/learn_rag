Albert Einstein was born on March 14, 1879, in Ulm, Germany.
He developed the theory of special relativity in 1905.
Einstein won the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1921.
He moved to the United States in 1933 to escape the Nazi regime.
Einstein worked at the Institute for Advanced Study in Princeton, New Jersey.
He died on April 18, 1955, in Princeton.
Einstein is widely regarded as one of the greatest physicists of all time.
His famous equation E=mc2 changed our understanding of energy and matter.
